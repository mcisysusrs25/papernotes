Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Elegant Notes, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

Information We Don't Collect

Elegant Notes is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process any of your notes or personal information. All your content remains on your device and is stored locally using your browser's localStorage feature.

Local Storage

Elegant Notes uses localStorage to save your notebooks and preferences. This data is stored directly on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties. The data remains on your device until you clear your browser's cache or localStorage.

Analytics and Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics tools to collect information about your usage of Elegant Notes.

Data Export

When you export your notes as PDF or DOCX files, this process happens entirely on your device. Your content is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers during this process.

Children's Privacy

Elegant Notes is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

How We Use Cookies

Elegant Notes does not use traditional cookies. Instead, we use localStorage, a similar technology that stores data directly in your browser without sending it to a server.

localStorage Usage

We use localStorage for the following purposes:

  • Storing your notebooks and their contents
  • Saving your preferences (such as font choice and focus mode settings)
  • Remembering which notebook you last accessed

Your Control Over localStorage

You can clear all data stored by Elegant Notes by clearing your browser's localStorage. This can typically be done through your browser's settings or privacy controls.

Third-Party Cookies

Elegant Notes does not use any third-party services that may set cookies.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Data Security

Our Approach to Data Security

At Elegant Notes, we take the security of your data seriously. Here's how we protect your information:

Local Storage Security

Elegant Notes stores all your notebooks and content in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We don't have access to your notes
  • Your data is subject to your browser's security measures

Export Security

When you export your notes as PDF or DOCX files, this process happens entirely on your device. Your content is not transmitted over the internet during export.

Limitations of localStorage

It's important to understand that localStorage has some limitations:

  • Data can be lost if you clear your browser cache or localStorage
  • Data is not synchronized across different devices
  • There are storage limits that vary by browser (typically 5-10MB)

Recommended Security Practices

For optimal security of your notes, we recommend:

  • Regularly export important notebooks as backup
  • Use a password-protected device
  • Keep your browser updated to benefit from the latest security features

If you have any questions about data security, please contact us at security@elegantnotes.com.
